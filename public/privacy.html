Privacy Policy — LootLoop

Last updated: July 2026

LootLoop ("we", "our", "the app") is a rewards application that lets users complete surveys, offers, and tasks from third-party partner networks in exchange for virtual coins redeemable for cash or other rewards. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to operate the app:

  • A randomly generated user ID: created automatically when you first open the app. It does not contain your name, email, or any personal identifier.
  • Coin balance and task completion history: stored so you can track your earnings.
  • Information shared with offerwall partners: when you open a survey or offer wall (such as CPX Research), that partner may independently collect information directly from you (such as demographic survey answers) under their own privacy policy. We do not control or see the content of third-party surveys.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or location.
  • We do not sell your data to advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To credit coins to your account when a partner network confirms you completed a task.
  • To let you track your balance and task history within the app.
  • To prevent fraud — for example, detecting duplicate or reversed task completions reported by our partners.

4. Third-Party Partners

LootLoop integrates with third-party offerwall and survey networks (currently CPX Research, with additional networks being added over time). When you interact with their offers, your device and any information you voluntarily provide to them is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. We recommend reviewing:

  • CPX Research: cpx-research.com/main/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

Your coin balance is stored in a secured database. Task completions are only credited after cryptographic verification (server-to-server postback with signature validation) — this also protects you from other users being able to fraudulently affect your balance.

6. Children's Privacy

LootLoop is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your local law), and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Your Choices

Since we do not collect personally identifying information, there is no personal account to delete. If you stop using the app, your locally stored data (balance, history) remains only on your device unless you contact us to request removal from our server-side database.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as LootLoop adds new features or partners. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
